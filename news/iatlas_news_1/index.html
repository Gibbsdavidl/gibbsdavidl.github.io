Introduction to iAtlas

The iAtlas portal serves as an interactive companion to the recently-to-be published paper “The Immune Landscape of Cancer”.

Basic Use

Currently, the portal is built using R/Shiny, which gives us a way to make quick prototypes and integrate feedback from users. Eventually, we will be moving towards a more robust javascript based solution.
